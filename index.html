Alhazen PDF ("we", "our" or "app") values your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information our mobile application collects, how it uses it, and how it protects it.

Alhazen PDF does not collect any personal data.

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since there is no data collection, all age groups can use it safely.
